About Chairman

Bijaya Khatri was born in holy city kathmandu Nepal who is the best known for her efforts to fight against sensitive issue such as curruption, poverty, child labour, domestic violence and promote government transparency. She is a founder of Bir Foundation Nepal and she is regarded as one of the best women who has done a great contribution towards a society...... Her main mission is to stop curruption and take out the people from poverty as well as provide good treatment to the poor people who can't afford. Her parents have had a major impact on her life. Her father worked as a secretary under Nepal government during king rules. Her mother taught her to be independent minded and wanted her to be a good nurse. She was studying nurse in Sanjeevani college Butwal but she realized that nurse was not her passion as she aimed to become a good social worker to finish the curruption and poverty in Nepal then she dropped out from the college. She got married earlier in small age. She became busy to take care of her family but she did not forget her passion and still was in her mind. She started a Non Government Organization BAS Nepal start her carrier as a member to Executive Director of BAS Rupandehi Nepal. She worked many places, specially Marchawar Rupandehi where most of the peoples were uneducated and doesn't knew about how to live life. Marchawar is located on the southern and western part of Nepal touches with UP India border. She worked with different organisations in Marchawar and changed the face of Marchawar coordinating with local leaders and different organisations. Now she create an organization name Bir Foundation and she is founder/president of it. Her aim is to open and establish a dental hospital in Marchawar and work non profitable and serve to those peoples who can not afford the medicine and treatment. Through this organisation she wants to make Marchawar healthy and educated.

About Foundation

Founded in 2020 by a young Nepali women named Bijaya Khatri at the age of 30, the BIR FOUNDATION NEPAL a registered non-governmental, non-profit organization aims to fight against sensitive issue such as curruption, poverty, child labour, domestic violence and promote government transparency. This may also mean rescuing the children from serving their parent's sentences and helping them enjoy their fundamental rights - food, clothes, shelter and education...... In nepal there is only few organizations that work to fight against sensitive issue such as curruption, poverty, child labour, domestic violence and promote government transparency. The need to provide a strong foundation for the construction of the nation by promoting transparency, fight against curruption, poverty, child labour and domestic violence, so early BIR FOUNDATION NEPAL was founded.
